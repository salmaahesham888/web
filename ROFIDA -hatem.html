Solar Chimney

Harnessing the Power of the Sun

A solar chimney is a structure that uses solar energy to generate electricity. It consists of a large glass collector at the base and a tall chimney in the center. As sunlight heats the air inside the collector, the hot air rises through the chimney. This rising air drives turbines placed at the base of the tower, generating electricity.
